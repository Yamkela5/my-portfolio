About Me

My name is Yamkela James. I was born in Cape Town on 20 May 1997. I grew up in Cape Town then relocated to Eastern Cape, where my mother grew up and I spent few years of my life there. I then moved back to Cape Town when I was going to do grade 7. I completed my matric in 2015 and I had nothing to do because I had no funding that would help me continue with my studies. I was introduced to programming in the beginning of 2017. I can say coding was the career I never planned. I started doing projects and it has been a great journey though it wasn't easy! My eagerness to learn is what helped me to keep on going though many times I felt like I was drowning.I loved what I was doing so much that I did not feel like giving up. Coding gave me a reason to wake up and keep pushing everyday and though it was not planned, I would not change it for anything!.
